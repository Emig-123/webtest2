About this data

Source measures replicate the workbook's Power Pivot model on NFCS_Master_Clean1: Pct Emergency Fund divides respondents with an emergency fund by all respondents who answered the question; Pct Too Much Debt divides respondents who strongly agree they have too much debt by all respondents in the slice, including non-responses (matching the workbook's DAX measure exactly); Avg Financial Satisfaction is a simple mean on the survey's 1–10 scale. Income Gap and Education Gap compare the top and bottom bracket of their respective dimension while ignoring that dimension's own filter, exactly as the original measures do.

  • The income and education breakdown charts intentionally ignore the income/education filter for their own dimension, since it is the chart's category axis — this mirrors how the source PivotTables were connected to their slicers.
  • Any slice with fewer than 5 respondents is treated as insufficient data rather than shown as a potentially unstable percentage.
  • All aggregation is computed client-side, in the browser, from the same 133,086-row extract used by the original PivotTables.
