About this data

Source measures replicate the workbook's Power Pivot model: fact_country_year (Gini coefficient and unemployment rate by country and year) related to DimCountry (region lookup) and DimYear; fact_macro supplies GDP per capita and advanced education % by country and year via the GDP per Capita and Advanced Education % measures. All aggregation shown here (yearly averages, regional min/max, scatter pairings) is computed client-side from the same row-level extract used by the original PivotTables — nothing is pre-rounded or hard-coded.

  • A country appears in a given year's scatterplots only if both series have a reported value for that exact year — matching the original CUBEVALUE-driven pivot behavior.
  • Gini coefficients are unitless (0–100 scale); higher values indicate greater income inequality.
